Faceswap

FaceSwap is a tool that utilizes deep learning to recognize and swap faces in pictures and videos.

Description

Move faces in an image to swap them. Set the points that match the face features, highlight the corresponding points, and move it to the right position. Rotate, zoom, and edit faces and tweak the parameters of the background picture. Apply automatic color adjustments.

FaceSwap has ethical uses

  • FaceSwap is not for creating inappropriate content.
  • FaceSwap is not for changing faces without consent or with the intent of hiding its use.
  • FaceSwap is not for any illicit, unethical, or questionable purposes.
  • FaceSwap exists to experiment and discover AI techniques, for social or political commentary, for movies, and for any number of ethical and reasonable uses.
